Welcome to our Hangman game!!

The rules is very simple:

  • We will give you a word and you will try to guess what it is.

  • At first, you should try to guess by letter, one by one (or you can guess the word immediately if you wanna go big LOL).

  • If you guess the word or a letter wrong, we will draw a part of the stickman on the hang.

  • If you manage to guess the word correctly before we draw a full stickman, you win.

  • If at some point, we have drawn the full stickman, but you still can't find the word, you lose.

    • Are you ready to start the game? I hope you do. Good luck :))
